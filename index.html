What is Nexus?

SmartCare is a centralized digital healthcare solution that tracks complete patient history, appointments, medical reports, doctor notes, and even provides emergency access for faster care delivery. It's built for doctors and patients alike with 3D data visualization, alerts, and real-time monitoring.

Core Features

🧑‍⚕️ Doctor & Patient Portal

Separate dashboards for doctors and patients to manage data in real-time.

📅 Appointments & Video Calls

Book, manage, and attend video consultations through a seamless system.

🔔 Smart Notifications

Get appointment reminders, emergency alerts, and health updates instantly.

📊 Health Analytics

Interactive 3D data visualizations and graphs for better health monitoring.

How It Works

Step 1

Signup using Email. Choose your role (Doctor or Patient).

Step 2

Complete profile and connect your health data securely.

Step 3

Access dashboards, book appointments, or manage records.

Step 4

Doctors can search history, write prescriptions & conduct calls.
